Project Overview

RAT EOS PC delivers a low-cost IoT platform that detects fire-risk conditions in real-time:

  • Deployment of 34 LoRaWAN gateways
  • 200 multisensor nodes to capture temperature, humidity, wind & CO₂ among other fields
  • Open-source stack technologies
  • Funded by EU Interreg POCTEP 2021-2027
  • www.rateospc.pt

System Architecture

  • Topology: Sensors → gateways → LoraWan servers → Mosquitto broker
  • Kafka buffers data
  • Logstash normalises according to FIWARE smart-data models.
  • Elasticsearch + Kibana power dashboards & maps.
  • N8N/ElastAlert push Slack / e-mail warnings & fire flags.

Alerting Workflow

  • Rules match in ElastAlert or N8N framework.
  • Alert context enriched (geo, sensor model).
  • Stakeholders notified in Slack & e-mail & Kibana Dashboards
